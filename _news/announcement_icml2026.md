One paper on [Tail-aware Flow Fine-Tuning (TFFT)](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.16796) accepted at **ICML 2026**! TFFT is a **risk-sensitive generative optimization** method that allows to efficiently seek novel samples (in molecular design) and control worst cases (in text-to-image generation)!
