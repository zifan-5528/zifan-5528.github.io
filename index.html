About me

I am currently a second-year Phd student affiliated with Division of Decision and Control Systems (DCS) at KTH Royal Institute of Technology. I am fortunate to jointly work with Prof. Karl H. Johansson at KTH and Prof. Michael M. Zavlanos at Duke University. Before that, I received both the master and bachelor degrees at Honors School of Harbin Institute of Technology under the supervision of Prof. Ligang Wu.

Research

My research interests lie broadly in the span of control theory, game theory, optimization and machine learning as well as their applications. Some of my recent research projects include risk-averse and distributional robust optimization.
